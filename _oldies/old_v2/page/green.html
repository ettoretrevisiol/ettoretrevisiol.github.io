  • master thesis: generalized nash equilibria for the service provisioning problem in multi-cloud systems

    this work captured the behavior of saas and iaas in conflicting situation during resources allocation in which the best strategy for one depends on the choices of the others. we then used game theory results to develop efficient algorithms for the run-time management and allocation of iaas resources to competing saass suitable for a fully distributed implementation.
    » keywords - game theory, generalized nash equilibria, cloud computing
    » advisor - prof. ardagna danilo
    » co-advisor - prof. mauro passacantando
    » download - pdf (email me for full version.)
